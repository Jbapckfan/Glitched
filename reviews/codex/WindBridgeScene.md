Level 2 — Microphone (blowing)

The wind bridge is understandable once the player sees the chasm and the "LOOKS WINDY" copy, though the first encounter still depends on associating wind with the microphone. The main chasm is outside Bit's jump reach by design, and the composed iPad path explicitly keeps ordinary hops under the 130-gap and 85-rise limits. The bridge retracts slowly enough that a first successful blow should not become a frame-perfect execution test. Deaths call `notePlayerStruggle()` and the later hint says to blow into the microphone, so stuck-player escalation exists. The rough edge is that microphone permission or denial is still a large hidden dependency, and ambient noise may unintentionally extend the bridge before the player understands why. One concrete improvement: after the first fall at the wind gap, add a small in-world mic/breath glyph beside the bridge so the clue escalates before the full text hint.
