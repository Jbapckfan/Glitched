Level 15 — Low Power Mode toggle

The level communicates "less power changes the world" without fully saying Low Power Mode, which is a fair first clue. Platforming appears safe by design: the composed iPad route uses bounded tiers, and the phone route's large obstacles are meant to be mode-gated rather than precision jumps. Deaths call `notePlayerStruggle()`, and first low-power activation records progress, so hint escalation is wired well. The main design problem is the always-present in-game power button, which can replace the real iOS Low Power toggle rather than merely rescuing players who cannot use it. That button also risks desynchronizing player expectations from the actual OS state, since tapping it changes the scene state, not iOS settings. One concrete improvement: hide the button until the fallback path is needed, and make the initial clue point at the real Settings/Control Center route.
