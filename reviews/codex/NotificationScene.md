Level 11 — Push notifications

The notification mechanic is fair but over-explained at t=0: "TAP THE BELL BUTTON ABOVE / THEN WAIT FOR THE ALERT" removes most of the initial deduction. The actual platform route is safe, with unjumpable locked doors doing the gating and iPad climb comments keeping gaps and rises within budget. Deaths call `notePlayerStruggle()`, and unlocking doors records progress, so the hint system has useful signals. The hint is functional but too short if notification permission is denied or the player missed the bell affordance. The roughest design edge is the decoy "SYSTEM" notification, which adds an OS-level gotcha on top of an already fragile external notification flow. One concrete improvement: delay the decoy until after the first successful notification interaction, or visually brand the genuine alert in the intro panel so the choice feels fair.
