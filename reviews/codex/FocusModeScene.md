Level 14 — Do Not Disturb / Focus Mode

The focus-mode idea is clear enough from "THE NOISE NEVER STOPS... UNLESS YOU LET IT...", though the visible fallback button risks telling the player this is a control toggle rather than a system-state puzzle. The jump route is not the hard part; hazards freezing and the exit latch carry the mechanic, while the platform rises stay within normal reach. Deaths call `notePlayerStruggle()`, and the later hint points to Focus/DND. The clue quality would be better if the fallback affordance were delayed until the player actually struggled or the OS state could not be observed. A rough edge is that enabling Focus does not appear to call `notePlayerProgress()`, so the shared hint timer may not reset at the key realization moment. One concrete improvement: record progress on the first Focus activation and only surface the fallback after a failed/unsupported system path.
