Level 13 — WiFi toggle

The WiFi rule is learnable from the signal art and "THE SIGNAL COMES AND GOES" clue, and the on/off platform contrast should be fair after one test. Geometry is safe where it is meant to be platforming, while the mode-specific platforms do the actual gating. The hint text names Control Center, but `handleDeath()` does not call `notePlayerStruggle()`, so repeated falls do not earn that hint faster. The major code/design mismatch is the download bar: comments say it is cosmetic and `handleExit()` succeeds without `downloadCompleted`, even though the guide expects completing a download to unlock the final section. That makes the level communicate an objective that the code does not enforce. One concrete improvement: either gate the final door on `downloadCompleted` or remove/rename the bar so players do not chase a fake requirement.
