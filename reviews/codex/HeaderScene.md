Level 1 — HUD drag

The mechanic is reasonably fair because the header is visually dominant and the opening text frames the HUD as the thing interfering, while avoiding a literal "drag the header down" spoiler. The first pit is deliberately too wide to jump, so the puzzle correctly forces the banner bridge rather than asking for platforming precision. The iPad route comments and platform sizing stay inside the 130-gap and 85-rise budgets, and the only large hole is the intended unjumpable problem. The clue escalation is better than average: deaths and failed drops call `notePlayerStruggle()`, and the hint eventually names dragging the top bar. The code's broad `dropInPlayBand && dropNearPit` acceptance makes the solution forgiving, but it also makes the bridge feel less like the player deliberately placed the header. One concrete improvement: after one miss, show a subtle grab affordance on the header and tighten the drop target slightly so success still feels intentional.
