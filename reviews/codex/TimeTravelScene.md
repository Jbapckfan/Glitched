Level 9 — App backgrounding (pressing Home / swiping up)

The "GROWTH TIME: 10 YEARS" sign is a good thematic clue and does not immediately spoil that backgrounding the app advances time. The tree/platform geometry is carefully described, with iPad tiers capped at 85 rise and branch hops kept within the 130-gap budget. The puzzle is fair once the player tries leaving, but the first encounter may read as waiting in place rather than backgrounding. The hint text eventually says to leave the app, but `handleDeath()` does not call `notePlayerStruggle()`, so failed platforming does not accelerate that reveal. The code's time-passage thresholds are otherwise robust because cumulative `gameYears` can still reach the ancient state across shorter absences. One concrete improvement: add a subtle "come back later" animation or narrator nudge after the player idles at the sapling without progress, and wire deaths into `notePlayerStruggle()`.
