Level 5 — Lightning cable / wireless charging

The charging mechanic is fair but heavily spoiled: the battery icon and "PLUG IN YOUR CHARGER" tell the player exactly what to do at t=0. The platforming looks safe, with the plug elevator handling the non-jump traversal and the iPad tiers staying within the 85-rise budget. The manual carry logic for Bit on the plug is a good completability safeguard, especially because the plug is not a normal dynamic platform. The hint text is explicit, but `handleDeath()` does not call `notePlayerStruggle()`, so repeated falls do not accelerate the hint and only the idle fallback can help. A rough edge is the hardcoded `190` surface reference in `animatePlugEntry`, which is brittle against the composed iPad start geometry. One concrete improvement: call `notePlayerStruggle()` on death and derive the plug burst/surface Y from the actual start platform instead of a literal.
