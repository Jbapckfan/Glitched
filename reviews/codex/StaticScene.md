Level 3 — Microphone (sustained noise)

The first half is clear, arguably too clear, because the panel says "MAKE NOISE / TO BLOCK LASERS" and removes most discovery. The inverse laser is fairer and more interesting, but "THIS ONE LISTENS DIFFERENTLY" is small enough that players may blame timing rather than infer the inverted rule. Platform geometry is straightforward and the documented rises and gaps stay comfortably inside Bit's reach; the lasers, not jumps, are the real gate. Deaths call `notePlayerStruggle()` and the hint eventually explains the dashed inverse behavior, so the escalation path is present. The code rough edge is that laser hit bodies are rectangular and not rotated to match arbitrary `start`/`end` beams, which is fine for vertical beams but brittle if content changes. One concrete improvement: make inverse lasers visually distinct through a persistent dashed shell or reversed meter, not only a tiny badge.
