Level 6 — Screen brightness slider

The mechanic is readable without being completely handed over: the dark floor, "BRIGHTNESS" sign, and upward arrow point toward the system slider while still asking the player to test it. The jump route is carefully bounded, with phone rises around 58-76 and iPad tiers capped within the 85-rise rule. The burn-at-max twist is fair after the player has learned that brightness affects the world, but it can be harsh if the device starts already near maximum brightness. Deaths call `notePlayerStruggle()`, and progress is recorded once brightness crosses a useful threshold. The rough edge is that a high initial `UIScreen.main.brightness` can activate burn zones before the player has had a clean teach moment. One concrete improvement: make the first over-bright burn a warning flash or short grace period until a platform has been successfully solidified once.
