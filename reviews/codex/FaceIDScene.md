Level 19 — Face ID / biometric authentication

The first instruction is extremely explicit: walk to the vault and tap it to scan your face, so the mechanic is fair but not mysterious. The platform route and two biometric blockers are built to prevent jump bypasses, with iPad rises and gaps documented inside the safe range. The multi-scan structure is less fair than the first gate because the final "one more scan" is only really taught when the player reaches the exit and is refused. Deaths do not call `notePlayerStruggle()`, and `hintText()` only says "Authenticate identity", which is less useful than the t=0 instruction. Code rough edges include several force unwraps around `secondDoor`/`secondDoorBlocker` construction, plus a fairly complex fallback path after repeated biometric declines. One concrete improvement: upgrade the hint to name the next gate/extra scan and call `notePlayerStruggle()` on death or repeated auth cancellation.
