Level 16 — Shake to undo (device shake gesture)

The rotten-platform collapse and just-in-time "SHAKE TO UNDO" prompt make the mechanic fair at the exact moment it is needed. The surrounding platform route stays inside safe jump limits, and the final trap is arranged so undo is required rather than bypassable. Deaths call `notePlayerStruggle()`, and repairing the trap records progress, so the hint system supports repeated failure. The t=0 clue is atmospheric enough that it does not spoil the shake gesture up front. The rough edge is resource design: `repairTrap()` does not consume an undo charge, which conflicts with the limited-undo premise and makes the forced solve feel exempt from the rule. One concrete improvement: have the trap repair consume a charge, then restore a minimum charge on respawn if needed to prevent softlock.
