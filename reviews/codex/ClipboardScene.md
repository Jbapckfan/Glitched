Level 12 — System clipboard (copy/paste)

The clipboard level has a strong discovery path: the terminal redacts the password, "EXTRACT & RETURN" points at moving data, and the hint eventually gives `GLITCH3D`. The locked door is tall enough to prevent a jump bypass, and the iPad route keeps the climb to safe one-tier steps. The t=0 text avoids directly saying "copy/paste", which is the right amount of restraint for this mechanic.
