The level is fair precisely because the t=0 text lies in an obvious enough way: "NO GIMMICK. JUST WALK" makes players suspicious without revealing the real exit behind them. The platforming route is real but audited, with filler platforms added so the far-right fake exit remains reachable on wider devices and iPad rises stay one tier at a time. Deaths call `notePlayerStruggle()`, and the hint "Are you sure the exit is ahead?" escalates the core suspicion cleanly. The real exit is hidden from VoiceOver until reveal, which preserves the twist for accessibility users instead of spoiling it. The rough edge is the hesitation tracker: it increments every two seconds of near-stillness and can inflate during confusion, cutscenes, or analysis pauses, making the post-reveal "trust" readout less meaningful. One concrete improvement: stop counting hesitations after reveal starts and only count while Bit is grounded and player control is active.
