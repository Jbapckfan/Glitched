Level 7 — Screenshot
The screenshot-freeze mechanic is a brilliant high-concept puzzle, but first-encounter fairness is weak because the instruction panel's "SOME MOMENTS REFUSE TO MOVE" line doesn't tell players how to freeze the bridge—they may try tapping or jumping. The completability math is sound: the bridge spans a 220pt chasm (wider than Bit's ~184pt jump reach), and the bridge is only solid during the freeze, so the mechanic is genuinely required. The hint quality is good: hintText() explicitly names the screenshot gesture, and the timer display gives immediate feedback on how much time remains. A specific code bug: bridgeGraceRemaining is set to 0.2 seconds when the timer expires while Bit is on the bridge, but dropBridge() is called immediately after the grace period—if Bit jumps during the grace window, the bridge may drop while he's in the air, causing a death that feels unfair. One concrete improvement: make the bridge's support cables glow brighter as the freeze timer runs out, and play a soft "tick" sound for the last 0.5 seconds, giving players a clear audio/visual warning that the bridge is about to drop.
