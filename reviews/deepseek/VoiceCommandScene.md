Level 22 — Voice Command
The ordered command chain (BRIDGE -> OPEN -> FLY) is well-structured, but first-encounter fairness is undermined by the instruction panel's "SPEAK TO YOUR PHONE" line—players may say any command out of order and get no feedback, leading to frustration. The completability math is sound: the bridge spans 227pt at the narrowest iPhone (exceeding Bit's ~184pt horizontal reach), the door is un-jumpable (107pt tall), and the FLY rise (100pt) exceeds the ~91pt jump apex, so each command is mechanically required. The hint quality is excellent: hintText() explicitly lists the correct order, and the fallback buttons appear after 6 seconds of silence. A specific code bug: flyUsed is set to true when FLY activates, but allCommandsSatisfied requires flyUsed even though FLY is the last command—this is correct, but bridgeExtended and doorOpened remain true after their timers expire, which could cause confusion if a player retriggers them. One concrete improvement: make the "SAY X" hints glow more brightly when the preceding command has been completed, guiding the player through the sequence without breaking the discovery.
