Level 21 — Device Name
The doppelganger race and name-gate doors create a compelling identity puzzle, but first-encounter fairness is poor because the instruction panel's "I KNOW WHO YOU ARE" doesn't tell players that the device name is the key—they may inspect the doors but not notice the labels changing. The completability math is sound: the bay pillars (10pt wide, 65pt tall) partition the final platform into three bays, and each bay is at least 26pt wide (27.2pt at 390w iPhone), so Bit can stand in any bay to trigger its door. The hint quality is good: hintText() reveals the device name connection, and the doppelganger's rejection at the wrong door visually teaches the puzzle. A specific code bug: resolvedFallbackRealX and resolvedFallbackDecoyX are set in buildPhoneLevel() but never updated when assignDoorIdentities() runs—if the real door isn't at the default center, the fallback positions are wrong. One concrete improvement: make the three door labels gradually fade in over 2 seconds after the name resolves, and highlight the matching door with a slow pulse, so players see which door is theirs without being handed the answer immediately.
