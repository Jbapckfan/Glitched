Level 5 — Charging
The plug-elevator is a clever hardware integration, but first-encounter fairness is compromised by the t=0 instruction panel's "I'M... FADING" line—players may think the battery icon is decorative and not realize that plugging in is the solution. The completability math is sound: the plug platform is non-dynamic and driven by SKAction/direct position writes, and Bit is manually carried by tracking plugIsRideable and adding the plug's per-frame deltaY to Bit's position, so the ride is smooth and reliable. The hint quality is excellent: hintText() explicitly tells players to "plug in your charger," and the dying-battery narration escalates appropriately. A specific code bug: plugIsRideable is set to true at the start of the animation, but lastTrackedPlugY is set at the same time—if the plug is moved by a parent transform (e.g., worldNode scaling), the deltaY tracking will be wrong. One concrete improvement: add a small plug icon that pulses on the battery indicator when the device is unplugged, and glows green when plugged in, giving players a clear, immediate visual cue about the state of the mechanic.
