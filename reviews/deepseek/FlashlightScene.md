Level 31 — Flashlight
The flashlight-and-tilt mechanic is a brilliant hardware integration, but first-encounter fairness is compromised because the instruction panel's "IT'S DARK DOWN HERE" line doesn't tell players that turning on the flashlight is the solution—they may think the dark is atmospheric. The completability math is sound: the light cone's shape changes with pitch (vertical = forward beam, flat = floor pool), and the stalactites/pits are only visible when the beam hits them, so the mechanic is genuinely required. The hint quality is good: hintText() explicitly names the flashlight, and the ambient glow ensures players can always see their feet. A specific code bug: updateCaveCreatures() removes fleeing creatures from the scene, but caveCreatures is cleared only when parent == nil—if a creature is removed by the flee action, the array still holds a reference until the next update pass, which could cause a retain cycle. One concrete improvement: add a soft "flashlight on" indicator (a small LED icon in the top-right HUD) that pulses when the flashlight is off, reminding players they have a tool they're not using.
