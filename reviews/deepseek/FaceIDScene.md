Level 19 — Face ID Gate
The multi-step biometric authentication is cleverly staged, but first-encounter fairness suffers because the instruction panel's "WALK TO THE VAULT" line doesn't explain what happens when you tap the door—players may tap repeatedly without understanding why the system prompt appears. The completability math is solid: door1's blocker height was increased to 150pt on iPad (from phone's 100pt) so its top sits 110pt above the platform top, clearing Bit's ~91pt apex by 19pt, making the gate genuinely un-jumpable. The hint quality is excellent: hintText() gives a clear "Authenticate identity" prompt, and the fallback (hasSurfacedAuthFallback) surfaces on-screen software buttons after two declined Face ID prompts. A specific rough edge: faceIDDeclineCount increments on every failed scan, but a player who accidentally cancels the system prompt twice will see the fallback even if they intended to use Face ID. One concrete improvement: make the on-screen fallback buttons pulse gently after the first declined scan, rather than waiting for two declines, so players who need assistive access discover them faster.
