Level 23 — Battery Percentage
The hidden-exit-below-50% mechanic is elegantly designed, but first-encounter fairness is weak because the instruction panel's "MORE POWER ISN'T ALWAYS BETTER" line doesn't signal that lowering battery is the goal—players may try to increase it instead. The completability math is sound: the hidden exit's exit body is inert (category .none) until battery < 60%, preventing bypass at high power, and the 10-stone visibility threshold creates a clear visual cue for which platforms are available. The hint quality is good: hintText() explicitly tells players to lower battery, and the DRAIN POWER button provides a simulator-friendly fallback. A specific code bug: simulatedBattery is initialized to currentBattery only when the drain button is tapped, but if the player never taps the button and the real battery never changes, the level is uncompletable on simulator—the fallback should auto-activate after 10 seconds on simulator builds. One concrete improvement: add a subtle visual cue (a pulsating arrow pointing downward from the 50% stone) that appears when battery > 60%, hinting that "something is hidden below" without explicitly naming the threshold.
