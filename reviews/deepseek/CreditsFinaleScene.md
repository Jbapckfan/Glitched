Level 30 — Credits Finale
The credits-as-platforms concept is charming, but first-encounter fairness is straightforward—climb up, avoid bugs. The completability math is sound: the vertical spacing between credit rungs is 76pt, well within Bit's ~91pt jump apex, and the zigzag lateral offset (±30pt with 110pt-wide rungs) ensures every hop is a simple vertical climb with minimal horizontal adjustment. The hint quality is basic but adequate: hintText() ("Climb the credits — watch for bugs!") matches the level's simplicity. A specific code bug: in buildComposedIPadLevel(), the signs array is never initialized, so the fourth-wall signs (placed in buildPhoneLevel()) are absent on the iPad path—they should be added in both branches. One concrete improvement: make the "YOU'RE STANDING ON THE PEOPLE WHO MADE ME" sign fade in only after the player has climbed past the halfway point, so it lands as a "oh, that's what this is" moment rather than an opening instruction.
