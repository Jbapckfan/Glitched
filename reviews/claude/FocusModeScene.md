STRENGTHS: Genuinely clever fourth-wall hook: the real OS Focus toggle is the mechanic, reinforced by a moon-lock door, calming white wash, and a "DO NOT DISTURB? I AM THE DISTURBANCE" narrator sting. iPhone layout is clean and the latch (hasFocusedOnce) means players cannot soft-lock by toggling Focus off after unlocking. CONCERNS: iPad is the main problem: the screenshot shows the course hugging the bottom-right quadrant with the entire upper-left ~60% as dead void, so the "full vertical band" claim in createHazardsComposedIPad fails visually.
