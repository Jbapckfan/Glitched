Reach on the degraded freezes is worryingly tight: the chasm is a deliberate 220 pt—well beyond Bit's 130 pt safe run-jump budget—and the final degraded freeze lasts only 1.0 s, which at moveSpeed 245 leaves no margin for acceleration or a mistimed jump. The iPad switchback climb mostly respects the budget (largest edge-to-edge gap ~125 pt, tier rises clamped to ≤85), but the high finale reuses the same 220 pt chasm with a long run-up, so the same timeout issue returns.
