Level 21 — Device Name
The doppelganger and decoy doors create an instantly recognizable identity test that feels genuinely rewarding to figure out on the first encounter. The iPad's switchback climb restricts all tier jumps to <=85pt, and the partition pillars successfully prevent the player from bypassing the door choice by jumping over them. The narrator's greeting and the "FIND THE DOOR WITH YOUR NAME" panel tease the mechanic perfectly without outright explaining the fake doors. However, the normalizedName fallback method uses a simple range(of:options:) to remove "'s ", which fails to account for names ending in "s'" (e.g., "James' iPhone") or those utilizing smart quotes, potentially leading to malformed labels on the final doors. A concrete improvement is to implement a more robust regex-based name extractor that handles smart quotes and trailing apostrophes to guarantee a clean display name.
