Level 32 — Multi-Touch Pressure
The requirement to hold multiple pressure plates simultaneously makes fantastic use of multi-touch without being overly punitive. The layout safely places the gates on a continuous safety ground, ensuring the player can't accidentally fall into a void while navigating the multi-touch logic. The "ONE OF YOU IS NOT ENOUGH" label sets the expectation perfectly, and the group evaluation naturally teaches the escalating finger requirements. A significant bug occurs if a system gesture (like swiping down Control Center) interrupts the game; SpriteKit may fail to deliver a touchesCancelled event, leaving a pressure plate permanently stuck in the active state. A concrete improvement is to add an active validation pass in updatePlaying to verify that all currently tracked touches still exist in the view.touches set, immediately deactivating any orphaned plates.
