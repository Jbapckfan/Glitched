Level 30 — Credits Finale
Navigating the scrolling credits as physical platforms serves as a wonderful, triumphant fake-out ending before the real conclusion. The 76pt vertical spacing strictly controls the jumps, remaining well under the 91pt maximum capability while the horizontal zig-zags stay safely compact. The "SAY THANK YOU" and "WATCH OUT FOR BUGS" labels provide excellent atmospheric flavor, reinforcing the meta nature of the finale. A code bug occurs when createBug assigns a scurrying moveBy action based entirely on a hardcoded duration and range; if the view is resized or the platform width changes dynamically, the bugs may scurry completely off their designated credit platform edges into mid-air. A concrete improvement is to dynamically calculate the moveBy distance using the actual width of the specific credit platform the bug is attached to, ensuring they never walk off the edge.
