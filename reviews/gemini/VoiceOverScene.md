Level 27 — VoiceOver
Phasing the path into existence upon triggering VoiceOver provides a brilliant visual and auditory puzzle that respects player accessibility by removing the need for blind platforming. The vertical tier steps are locked securely to <=85pt, and the decoy placements force careful reading without creating impossible jumps. "SOLID BEARS WEIGHT. BARRED LIES." gives precisely the right amount of information to navigate the decoys without babying the player. A rough edge is that if the player turns VoiceOver on and off rapidly, the dimRevealedState and showRevealedState methods can conflict on the stone.surface alpha values, causing real platforms to temporarily appear faded due to overlapping SKAction animations. A concrete improvement is to call removeAllActions() on the stone surfaces and glyphs before applying the new alpha states to prevent animation overlap during rapid toggles.
