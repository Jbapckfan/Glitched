Level 23 — Battery Percentage
Tying platform visibility to the battery percentage is established early, and the hidden exit trick is an elegant application of this logic that rewards observation. The layout carefully clusters the platforms to maintain gap thresholds, and dropping from the 50% plateau to the hidden exit provides a safe, controlled fall. The battery drain visual is effectively communicated by the dimming lights and subtle OS commentary, while the hint text safely guides struggling players. A rough edge in updateBatteryVisuals is that it continually assigns a new color to dimOverlay based on the battery level, but because it doesn't cross-fade or animate the color change, rapid simulated battery drains cause jarring, instant shifts in screen brightness. A concrete improvement is to apply the fill color change to the dimOverlay using a brief SKAction.colorize or custom fade interpolation to ensure smooth lighting transitions.
