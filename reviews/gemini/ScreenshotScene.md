Level 7 — Screenshot
Freezing the ghost bridge with a screenshot is a brilliant literal interpretation of capturing a moment in time. The 220pt chasm ensures the bridge is absolutely necessary, and the layout respects the jump and height maximums on all devices. The "SOME MOMENTS REFUSE TO MOVE" text serves as an excellent poetic clue before the timer forces the explicit revelation. A rough edge occurs in freezeBridge: if the user takes another screenshot while the bridge is already frozen, it resets frozenTimeRemaining but fails to reset the flicker warning animations properly, causing the bridge to incorrectly blink even though it has plenty of time left. A concrete improvement is to properly reset the bridge's alpha to 1.0 and remove any active flicker actions when extending an active freeze via a consecutive screenshot.
