Level 19 — Face ID Gate
The multi-step scanning process and the doppelganger race provide a brilliant twist on identity verification that feels both fair and highly engaging. The vertical tier spacing keeps platform ascents under 85pt, while the un-jumpable 150pt blocker properly gates progression to ensure the puzzle cannot be bypassed. Initial hints encourage scanning without spoiling the fake-door twist, and failure gracefully scales up to full disclosure of the solution. A rough edge exists in the resolveFallbackName() method; while it safely extracts the owner's name, if UIDevice.current.name uses a smart quote (e.g., ') instead of a straight apostrophe, the method fails to trim the possessive, displaying awkward text like "Usuario's". A concrete improvement is to update the fallback name extraction to check for both straight and smart quotes (or use a regular expression) to ensure the display name is consistently clean.
