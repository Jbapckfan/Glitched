Level 10 — Orientation
Widening the corridor by physically rotating the device to landscape is a straightforward yet incredibly satisfying "aha" moment. The layout places the crusher and exit within easily walkable distances on a continuous floor, requiring no complex platforming jumps. The "UP IS A MATTER OF OPINION..." clue and the dizzy commentary reward exploration and explicitly guide the player when they struggle. A visual bug occurs in updateWorldScale where the logic animates the xScale and yScale of worldNode over 0.5s; because physicsBody scales don't interpolate smoothly in SpriteKit, collision detection can behave erratically and trap the player during the rotation animation. A concrete improvement is to momentarily disable collision on the player or pause physics interactions during the 0.5s orientation animation, re-enabling them only once the scale has settled.
