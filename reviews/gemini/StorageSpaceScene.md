Level 24 — Storage Space
The floor-to-ceiling junk mass effectively forces the player to engage the terminal, turning an abstract cache-clear concept into a tangible physical objective. The platform gaps are all kept under 130pt, and the jump to the terminal ledge is an easily reachable 60pt rise, making the level consistently completable. The "JUNK MASS WALLS OFF THE EXIT" message successfully explains the obstruction without spoiling the settings-menu purge trick. A rough edge occurs during dissolveJunkMass, where the junk blocks run an action to move and scale down over 0.8 seconds before being removed from the parent, but their physics bodies aren't disabled until the animation completes, which can unpredictably collide with the player in mid-air. A concrete improvement is to immediately set the physics body of the junk blocks to nil at the start of the dissolveJunkMass sequence so they don't block or bounce the player during their scatter animation.
