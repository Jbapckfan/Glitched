Level 25 — Time of Day
The cycle between day, night, and secret mode is visually striking, making the requirement to bypass the sleeping guard intuitive and fair. The hand-composed vertical climb ensures that every jump to the next tier is within the 85pt rise limit and horizontally traversable. The atmospheric "THE WORLD CHANGES WITH THE CLOCK" offers a strong thematic clue without prematurely revealing the "Cycle Time" toggle strategy. A specific bug is that when toggling back to day mode, the enemy movement action uses SKAction.moveBy and snaps the enemy back to its origin immediately before restarting the patrol; if a switch occurs mid-move, this causes the enemy to teleport abruptly and potentially hit the player unfairly. A concrete improvement is to smoothly ease the enemy back to its origin over a short duration, or simply pause and unpause the existing action in place rather than completely resetting its position.
