Level 20 — Delete to Win
Presenting the level as a corrupted, impassable wall alongside a simulated app-delete purge is a striking, fair fourth-wall break that fits the meta theme perfectly. The layout places the exit 130pt away from the approach platform, keeping the final post-purge jump within Bit's reach constraints. The ominous "TERMINAL" warning and "IT WANTS YOU TO TURN BACK" text hint at the required physical collision without handing away the simulated crash sequence. A specific bug occurs when showFakeCrashScreen attempts to clean up the crash overlay by iterating over gameCamera?.enumerateChildNodes; if the camera has not been fully initialized or attached during the transition, this optional chaining fails silently, leaving the black overlay permanently blocking the screen. A concrete improvement would be to assign the crash overlay to a dedicated, guaranteed HUD container node rather than relying on the camera's state for critical visual cleanup.
