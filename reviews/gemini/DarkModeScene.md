Level 8 — Dark Mode
Toggling Dark Mode to shift the solidity of the platforms is a mind-bending puzzle that cleanly maps UI state to physical reality. The alternating sun/moon platforms maintain reachable horizontal jumps of less than 130pt and vertical ascents of exactly one safe tier. The "WHAT HOLDS YOU IN ONE IS A RUMOR IN THE OTHER" panel is fantastic atmospheric storytelling, supported by the explicit fail-state clue. A notable code edge case occurs when updateDualPlatforms alters the categoryBitMask to zero out invisible platforms; if the player is perfectly aligned inside the bounding box of a platform as it becomes solid, they can become permanently trapped inside it. A concrete improvement is to ensure that a platform only regains its solid physics body if it does not currently intersect with the player's bounding box, delaying solidity until the player clears the area.
