Level 33 — App Store Review
Feigning an App Store review request as the ultimate obstacle provides a brilliant comedic payoff that respects the player's agency. The flat layout correctly prevents the 120pt tall door from being jumped over, enforcing interaction with the fake review button. The lengthy terminal monologue provides all the context needed, making it clear that the validation request is a joke rather than a real wall. A specific bug is found in the shatterPadlock method, which creates fragment pieces with random initial velocities but fails to assign physical collision bounds, causing the padlock pieces to fall straight through the ground platform rather than bouncing. A concrete improvement is to assign standard dynamic physics bodies to the padlock fragments so they bounce satisfyingly against the floor before fading out.
