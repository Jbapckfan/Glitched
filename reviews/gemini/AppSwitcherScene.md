Level 18 — App Switcher Peek
The freeze effect paired with the drawing of trajectory lines communicates the app switcher peek excellently without unfairly killing the player on their first glance. The zigzag column cleanly structures the iPad path, keeping rises clamped to the safe maxJumpableRise and edge-to-edge gaps well below 130pt. The atmospheric "THE WORLD HOLDS ITS BREATH" avoids early spoilers, gracefully giving way to the literal swipe-up hint upon repeated player failure. However, the trajectory line drawing creates many small, overlapping line segments using a while loop inside showTrajectoryLines, which is computationally expensive and can cause frame drops if recreated frequently during rapid peeks. A concrete improvement is to replace the loop-based dashed trajectory generation with a single SKShapeNode path using CGPath(dashingWithPhase:lengths:) for significantly better rendering performance.
