Level 5 — Charging
The physical plugging in of the device to summon the giant elevator plug is an intuitive, magical hardware interaction. The iPad's new gutter climb properly fills the vertical void while keeping all jumps within the 130pt/85pt limits before reaching the central plug. The "FEED ME" narration sets the dying battery tone perfectly, smoothly escalating to the explicit hint upon player failure. A severe code bug exists in the plugPlatformCurrentY logic inside updatePlaying: it limits the sinking rate using deltaTime, but because it lacks a hard cap on deltaTime itself, an app suspended in the background could cause a massive deltaTime spike that drops the platform out of bounds instantly when the app resumes. A concrete improvement is to cap the maximum deltaTime processed in a single frame to a reasonable maximum (e.g., 0.1 seconds) to prevent physics and positional logic from breaking during app backgrounding or lag spikes.
